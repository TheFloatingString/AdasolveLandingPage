About Adasolve

Our goal is to increase the efficency of cancer diagnosis and treatment.

We are a platform that provides free cancer diangostics to individual by using AI to analyze the components of blood samples. In return, individuals can readily get in touch with clinics, health professionals or researchers on the Adasolve platform at with high accessibility to further assess their personnalized portfolios.

Method

We use deep learning and related data mining tools to deliver personalized diagnostics.


Our current milestones:

  • Improved accuracy of detecting cancer based on ctDNA data from a John Hopkins University paper from 60% to 92%.
  • Analyzed individual importance of different biomarkers.
  • Offers custom crafted AI diagnostics to predict the occurence of general cancer.
